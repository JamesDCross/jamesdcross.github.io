Treasure iLand

Play the prototype

How to Play

Move with the arrow keys
Press F to dig

Elevator Pitch

A hot and cold game with a pirate theme, you must race against the clock and find all the chests.

Concept

The player digs for treasure on a desert island. Chests have different depths, most contain gold but some contain special crackers. These allow Polly (an so the player) to see all chests or provide a time bonus the player can use at their disposal. When all the chests in all levels on an island are cleared the player progresses to the next island. Some islands contain ruins some contain other pirates... and maybe an easter egg or two, 4 8 15 16 23 42 anyone?
