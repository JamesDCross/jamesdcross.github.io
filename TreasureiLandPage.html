Elevator Pitch

A hot and cold game with a pirate theme, where you race against the clock to dig up more treasure.

Concept

The player digs for treasure on a desert island chests have different depths most contain gold but some contain “exotic loot”. The player uses the gold to buy crackers for Polly his parrot. Polly needs constant feeding to maintain his memory of where the chests are. Some chests also contain special crackers that allow Polly (and so the player) to see all chests or provide a time bonus. The player can use the gold they collect to build their ship which lets them travel to new and exciting islands. Some islands contain ruins some contain other pirates who are also after treasure.
